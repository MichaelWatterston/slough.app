Slough App is the community app built exclusively for Slough. Discover what's happening on your street, buy and sell with people nearby, find jobs close to home, and join groups that match your interests. All in one place, made for our town.

See what's happening on your street

Real-time posts, photos and videos from neighbours across the town. No noise from anywhere else — just Slough.

Buy and sell with people nearby

Skip the postage and the strangers from miles away. List in seconds, message safely in-app, and collect somewhere you already know.

Find work close to home

Browse roles from Slough employers and side gigs from local residents. Apply directly in the app and skip the commute.

Join groups and meet your people

Fourteen interest categories — sport, families, food, faith, parenting, hobbies and more. Find your tribe in Slough and start showing up.
